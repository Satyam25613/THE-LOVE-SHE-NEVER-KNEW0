About Us

Welcome to SR Books!

At SR Books, we believe in the power of stories that resonate with the heart and soul. Our journey began with the simple yet profound idea of bringing timeless, emotional stories to life. Our mission is to create literary works that inspire, connect, and speak to readers on a deeply personal level.

Our Story

“The Love She Never Knew” is more than just a book — it’s an emotional journey, a narrative of unspoken love, loss, and the beauty of selfless friendship. Born out of real college experiences, the story captures moments of deep emotions, cherished memories, and the bittersweet essence of unexpressed feelings.

The author, Satyam Singh Rajput, started writing with the idea of expressing the silent emotions we all carry and experience in our own lives. From the very first page to the last, the book seeks to touch hearts and leave a lasting impact.

What Makes Us Unique

At SR Books, we don’t just sell books — we offer a connection. Whether you're reading “The Love She Never Knew” as a casual reader or as someone reflecting on their own experiences, we want to provide a reading experience that feels like it's speaking directly to you.
